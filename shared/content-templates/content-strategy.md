# PostPilot: Content Strategy Overview

## 1. Tone & Voice Guidelines

| Platform | Tone | Key Characteristics |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Instagram** | Aesthetic & Educational | Visually driven, friendly, lifestyle-oriented, using emojis and relatable language. |
| **LinkedIn** | Professional & Thought-Leader | Authoritative yet approachable, value-driven, focused on results, career growth, and industry insights. |
| **Twitter/X** | Punchy & Real-Time | Short, sharp, conversational, often uses threads for deep dives, personality-forward. |

---

## 2. Hashtag Strategy

### Categories
- **Industry:** Broad keywords (#DigitalMarketing, #SaaS, #HealthAndWellness)
- **Community:** Specific group keywords (#SoloEntrepreneurs, #ReactDevs, #YogaLife)
- **Trending:** Timely topics or challenges (#MondayMotivation, #BuildInPublic)
- **Branded:** Unique to the user (#PostPilotApp, #YourBrandName)

### Platform Mix
- **Instagram:** 5-10 hashtags. Mix: 2 Industry, 4 Community, 2 Trending, 1-2 Branded.
- **LinkedIn:** 3-5 hashtags. Mix: 2 Industry, 2 Community, 1 Branded.
- **Twitter/X:** 1-2 hashtags. Mix: 1 Trending or 1 Industry.

## 3. Weekly Content Calendar Template

| Day | Post Type | Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Monday** | Educational / Value | Establish authority with a tip or "how-to". |
| **Tuesday** | Engagement / Myth Buster | Spark conversation by challenging a common belief. |
| **Wednesday** | Behind-the-Scenes / Personal | Build trust by showing the process or a personal lesson. |
| **Thursday** | Promotional / Social Proof | Showcase results, testimonials, or the product. |
| **Friday** | Reflection / Community | Wrap up the week and ask the audience about their wins. |

## 4. Post Templates

### Instagram Templates

#### IG1: The Educational Carousel
- **Structure:** Slide 1: Hook; Slides 2-4: Steps/Tips; Slide 5: Recap; Slide 6: CTA.
- **Length:** Caption < 500 characters.
- **Visuals:** High-quality graphics or text overlays on brand colors.
- **Posting Time:** 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM local time.

#### IG2: The Relatable Reel
- **Structure:** 3-second hook; 7-10 seconds of "vibe" or quick tip; Final screen CTA.
- **Length:** Caption < 200 characters.
- **Visuals:** Short-form video with trending audio.
- **Posting Time:** 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM local time.

#### IG3: The Aesthetic Quote
- **Structure:** High-impact quote graphic; Caption expands on the quote's meaning.
- **Length:** Caption 300-600 characters.
- **Visuals:** Minimalist graphic with elegant typography.
- **Posting Time:** 12:00 PM (Lunch break).

---

### LinkedIn Templates

#### LI1: The "Scroll-Stopper" Text Post
- **Structure:** One-sentence hook; Line break; 3-5 bullet points of value; CTA question.
- **Length:** 1,000 - 1,500 characters.
- **Visuals:** None (Text-only is highly effective) or a professional headshot.
- **Posting Time:** 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM (Workday start).

#### LI2: The Insightful PDF Document
- **Structure:** Slide-based PDF sharing data or a case study.
- **Length:** Caption < 400 characters.
- **Visuals:** 5-10 slide PDF.
- **Posting Time:** Tuesday/Thursday 10:00 AM.

#### LI3: The Professional Win
- **Structure:** Story of a recent success; What was learned; Who helped; CTA Tagging others.
- **Length:** 800-1,200 characters.
- **Visuals:** Photo of the team or the result.
- **Posting Time:** Friday 2:00 PM.

---

### Twitter/X Templates

#### TW1: The "Hot Take"
- **Structure:** Contrarian opinion or bold statement; 1-2 sentences.
- **Length:** < 280 characters.
- **Visuals:** None.
- **Posting Time:** Any time (High frequency).

#### TW2: The Educational Thread
- **Structure:** Tweet 1: Hook + "Thread 🧵"; Tweets 2-6: Value points; Final Tweet: CTA.
- **Length:** 5-7 tweets total.
- **Visuals:** Optional chart or image in the first tweet.
- **Posting Time:** 9:00 PM (Good for deep reading).

#### TW3: The Community Poll
- **Structure:** Question; 3-4 options; "Why did you pick this?" in the text.
- **Length:** Short and sweet.
- **Visuals:** Poll widget.
- **Posting Time:** Wednesday 11:00 AM.
