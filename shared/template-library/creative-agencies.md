# Creative Agency & Freelancer Templates

## Instagram (IG)

### CREATIVE_IG_1: The Portfolio Piece
- **Hook:** "New work for [Client Name]! 🎨"
- **Body:** Briefly explain the brief and the creative solution provided.
- **CTA:** "Check out the full case study on our site!"
- **Visual:** High-quality carousel showing the final design/video/project.

### CREATIVE_IG_2: The Workspace
- **Hook:** "Where the magic happens. ✨"
- **Body:** Show your setup and the tools you use to stay creative.
- **CTA:** "What's one thing you can't live without in your office?"
- **Visual:** An aesthetic "desk setup" photo or video.

### CREATIVE_IG_3: Design Tip
- **Hook:** "3 ways to improve your [Skill, e.g., Logo Design] instantly! 💡"
- **Body:** List 3 quick, actionable design tips.
- **CTA:** "Save this for your next creative project! 💾"
- **Visual:** A graphic with text overlay.

## LinkedIn (LI)

### CREATIVE_LI_1: Client Collaboration
- **Hook:** "The secret to a successful project? Communication."
- **Body:** Discuss how to manage client expectations and provide a great experience.
- **CTA:** "How do you handle client feedback? Let's share some tips."
- **Visual:** A photo of you and a client in a meeting or on a Zoom call.

### CREATIVE_LI_2: The Value of Design
- **Hook:** "Good design is good business. Here's why."
- **Body:** Explain the ROI of professional branding and creative work.
- **CTA:** "Is your brand ready for a refresh? Let's chat."
- **Visual:** A professional before/after brand refresh graphic.

### CREATIVE_LI_3: Scaling a Creative Business
- **Hook:** "How I went from solo freelancer to a small agency team."
- **Body:** Share the challenges and rewards of hiring and delegating.
- **CTA:** "Are you a solo creator or part of a team? Drop a comment."
- **Visual:** A photo of your team or your office space.

---

## Twitter/X (TW)

### CREATIVE_TW_1: The Design Roast (Lighthearted)
- **Hook:** "Comic Sans walk into a bar..."
- **Body:** A quick design joke or a hot take on a current trend.
- **CTA:** "Don't @ me. 😂 RT if you agree."
- **Visual:** None.

### CREATIVE_TW_2: The Tools Thread
- **Tweet 1:** "5 tools every freelancer needs in 2026. 🧵"
- **Body:** A series of tweets mentioning Notion, Figma, Stripe, etc.
- **CTA:** "What's in your stack?"
- **Visual:** None.

### CREATIVE_TW_3: Seeking Feedback
- **Hook:** "Working on something new. Thoughts on this color palette?"
- **Body:** Show 2-3 options.
- **CTA:** "A or B? Let me know! 👇"
- **Visual:** An image showing the options.
