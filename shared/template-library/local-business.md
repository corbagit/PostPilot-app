# Local Business Content Templates (Cafes, Salons, Local Shops)

## Instagram (IG)

### LOCAL_IG_1: The Product/Service "Vibe"
- **Hook:** "Your morning just got an upgrade. ☕✨" (Adjust based on business type)
- **Body:** Describe the sensory experience of the product or service. Why is it the perfect treat today?
- **CTA:** "Stop by and see us! We're open until [Time]."
- **Visual:** A high-quality, aesthetic photo of the product or a client enjoying the service.

### LOCAL_IG_2: Meet the Team / Behind the Scenes
- **Hook:** "The faces behind your favorite [Product/Service]. 👋"
- **Body:** Introduce a staff member or show the prep work that goes into the day.
- **CTA:** "Say hi to [Name] next time you're in!"
- **Visual:** A warm, friendly photo of the team or a video of the "opening" ritual.

### LOCAL_IG_3: Customer Review / Shoutout
- **Hook:** "We love our community! ❤️"
- **Body:** Share a glowing review from a regular or a photo of a happy customer in the shop.
- **CTA:** "Leave us a review and let us know your favorite [Product/Service]!"
- **Visual:** A graphic featuring the review text or a photo of the customer (with permission).

## LinkedIn (LI)

### LOCAL_LI_1: Business Milestone / Growth
- **Hook:** "5 years in [City Name]! Looking back on where we started."
- **Body:** Share the story of starting the local business, the challenges overcome, and gratitude for the local community.
- **CTA:** "Support local! What's your favorite small business in town?"
- **Visual:** A side-by-side "then and now" photo of the storefront.

### LOCAL_LI_2: Community Impact / Partnership
- **Hook:** "Proud to partner with [Local Charity/Event] this weekend."
- **Body:** Discuss the importance of local businesses supporting their communities and how you're giving back.
- **CTA:** "Learn more about the event at the link in our bio."
- **Visual:** A photo of the team volunteering or a graphic for the event.

### LOCAL_LI_3: Hiring / Company Culture
- **Hook:** "We're growing! Come join the [Business Name] family."
- **Body:** Highlight what makes working for your local business unique (flexibility, team vibe, community connection).
- **CTA:** "Tag someone who would be a great fit or apply at the link in bio!"
- **Visual:** A group photo of the current team looking happy and engaged.

## Twitter/X (TW)

### LOCAL_TW_1: The Daily Special / Quick Update
- **Hook:** "Today's Special: [Item Name]! 🥑🍞"
- **Body:** Short, punchy description of a deal or a new arrival.
- **CTA:** "Available until sold out! Come grab one."
- **Visual:** A quick photo of the special.

### LOCAL_TW_2: The Community Question
- **Hook:** "Early birds or night owls: When do you prefer to [Action, e.g., get your hair done]?"
- **Body:** Simple question to engage local followers.
- **CTA:** "Vote below! 👇"
- **Visual:** None (Use poll widget).

### LOCAL_TW_3: Local Event Shoutout
- **Hook:** "Good luck to everyone running the [Local Race] today! 🏃‍♂️💨"
- **Body:** Showing support for local events or other businesses.
- **CTA:** "We'll be open for post-race [Product]!"
- **Visual:** None or a simple emoji-filled tweet.
