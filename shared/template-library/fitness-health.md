# Fitness & Health Content Templates

## Instagram (IG)

### FITNESS_IG_1: The Workout Circuit
- **Hook:** "Burn [Goal] with this 15-minute circuit! 🔥"
- **Body:** List 4-5 exercises with reps/sets.
- **CTA:** "Save this for your next session! 💾"
- **Visual:** Video reel of the workout or a multi-slide carousel.

### FITNESS_IG_2: Transformation Tuesday
- **Hook:** "Progress isn't linear. 📈"
- **Body:** Tell the story of a client's journey, focusing on mindset as much as physical change.
- **CTA:** "DM 'START' to join the waitlist."
- **Visual:** Split-screen before/after or a photo of the client smiling.

### FITNESS_IG_3: Healthy Recipe
- **Hook:** "Eat this, not that: [Recipe Name] 🥗"
- **Body:** List ingredients and a quick 3-step method.
- **CTA:** "Tag a friend who loves [Main Ingredient]!"
- **Visual:** High-quality food photography or a quick cooking video.

## LinkedIn (LI)

### FITNESS_LI_1: Corporate Wellness
- **Hook:** "Why your fitness is your greatest business asset."
- **Body:** Discuss the link between physical activity, mental clarity, and productivity.
- **CTA:** "How do you stay active during a busy workday?"
- **Visual:** Photo of you working out in professional gear or at a standing desk.

### FITNESS_LI_2: The Science of Habit
- **Hook:** "Motivation is fleeting. Systems are forever."
- **Body:** Breakdown 3 psychological tips for sticking to a routine (e.g., habit stacking).
- **CTA:** "What's one habit you've mastered this year?"
- **Visual:** A clean, educational infographic.

### FITNESS_LI_3: Leadership through Discipline
- **Hook:** "Lessons from the gym that apply to the boardroom."
- **Body:** Comparison between training for a marathon and scaling a company.
- **CTA:** "What has sport taught you about leadership?"
- **Visual:** Professional photo of you in an athletic setting.

## Twitter/X (TW)

- **Hook:** "Don't wait for Monday."
- **Body:** Punchy 1-2 sentences to get people moving.
- **CTA:** "Go do 10 pushups right now. RT when done."
- **Visual:** None.

### FITNESS_TW_2: The Myth Buster

### FITNESS_TW_3: Weekly Wins

- **Visual:** None.
