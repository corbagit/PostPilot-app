# E-commerce & Retail Content Templates

## Instagram (IG)

### ECOM_IG_1: Product Drop
- **Hook:** "The wait is finally over! [Product Name] is here. ✨"
- **Body:** Highlight the best feature and why it's a must-have.
- **CTA:** "Shop now via link in bio! 🛒"
- **Visual:** High-quality studio photo or video of the product.

### ECOM_IG_2: User-Generated Content (UGC)
- **Hook:** "Spotted! [Customer Name] looking stunning in [Product]. 😍"
- **Body:** Share how customers are using/wearing the product in real life.
- **CTA:** "Tag us to be featured! #YourBrandTag"
- **Visual:** A customer's photo or a carousel of UGC.

### ECOM_IG_3: Behind-the-Scenes (BTS)
- **Hook:** "Pack an order with us! 📦"
- **Body:** Show the care and effort that goes into every shipment.
- **CTA:** "What are you ordering next?"
- **Visual:** A fast-paced Reel of the packing process.

## LinkedIn (LI)

### ECOM_LI_1: Scaling a Brand
- **Hook:** "From $0 to $[X] in [Timeframe]: The lessons I learned."
- **Body:** Discuss inventory management, customer acquisition, or logistics.
- **CTA:** "What's the biggest challenge in your e-commerce journey?"
- **Visual:** A professional chart showing growth or a photo of your warehouse.

### ECOM_LI_2: The Future of Retail
- **Hook:** "Is brick-and-mortar dead? Not according to these 3 trends."
- **Body:** Analysis of omni-channel retail and the importance of customer experience.
- **CTA:** "How is your brand adapting to the digital-first era?"
- **Visual:** A professional infographic about retail trends.

### ECOM_LI_3: Supply Chain Transparency
- **Hook:** "Why we chose [Material/Provider] for our products."
- **Body:** Discuss ethics, quality control, and the value of a transparent supply chain.
- **CTA:** "Do you prioritize sustainability when you shop? Let's discuss."
- **Visual:** A photo of the materials or the production process.

## Twitter/X (TW)

### ECOM_TW_1: The Flash Sale
- **Hook:** "FLASH SALE! ⚡"
- **Body:** [X]% off for the next [X] hours only! Use code: [CODE].
- **CTA:** "Shop the link in our bio before it's gone!"
- **Visual:** A bright, eye-catching 'SALE' graphic.

### ECOM_TW_2: Customer Service
- **Hook:** "Got a question about your order?"
- **Body:** We're here to help! Our team is online and ready.
- **CTA:** "DM us for any inquiries. 💬"
- **Visual:** None.

### ECOM_TW_3: Product Teaser
- **Hook:** "Something big is coming... 🤫"
- **Body:** A blurred image or a cryptic clue about the next launch.
- **CTA:** "Guess what it is in the replies!"
- **Visual:** A teaser image or video.
