# SaaS & Tech Content Templates

## Instagram (IG)

### SAAS_IG_1: The Feature Spotlight
- **Hook:** "Stop doing [Problem] the hard way. 🛑"
- **Body:** Briefly explain how [Feature Name] solves the problem in 3 simple steps.
- **CTA:** "Link in bio to try it for free!"
- **Visual:** High-quality screenshot of the feature or a short screen recording.

### SAAS_IG_2: The Founder's Journey
- **Hook:** "Why we built [Product Name]... 🚀"
- **Body:** Share a vulnerable story about the "aha!" moment or a challenge faced during development.
- **CTA:** "Follow for more behind-the-scenes!"
- **Visual:** Candid photo of the founder or team in the office.

### SAAS_IG_3: Customer Success Quote
- **Hook:** "Results speak louder than words. 📈"
- **Body:** A punchy testimonial from a happy user.
- **CTA:** "Read the full case study via link in bio."
- **Visual:** Aesthetic quote graphic with brand colors.

## LinkedIn (LI)

### SAAS_LI_1: Industry Insight
- **Hook:** "The [Industry] landscape is changing. Here's why you should care."
- **Body:** 3-5 bullet points analyzing a current trend and how your SaaS fits into the future.
- **CTA:** "What's your take on this? Let's discuss in the comments."
- **Visual:** A professional chart or infographic.

### SAAS_LI_2: The Product Update
- **Hook:** "Big news! [Feature] is officially live. 🎉"
- **Body:** Explain the value proposition and how it helps professionals save time/money.
- **CTA:** "Book a demo today."
- **Visual:** High-res image of the new UI or a 'New Feature' banner.

### SAAS_LI_3: Thought Leadership
- **Hook:** "[Controversial Opinion] is a myth."
- **Body:** Challenge a common industry belief and provide an alternative perspective based on your data.
- **CTA:** "Agree or disagree? Drop a comment."
- **Visual:** Professional headshot of the founder/CEO.

---

## Twitter/X (TW)

### SAAS_TW_1: The Quick Tip
- **Hook:** "Pro tip for [Target Audience]:"
- **Body:** One sentence of actionable advice.
- **CTA:** "RT if you needed this today."
- **Visual:** None or a small GIF.

### SAAS_TW_2: The Thread (Tutorial)
- **Tweet 1:** "How to [Goal] in 5 minutes using [Product Name]. 🧵"
- **Body:** A series of 5-7 tweets breaking down a workflow.
- **CTA:** "Subscribe to our newsletter for more tips."
- **Visual:** Image in the first tweet.

### SAAS_TW_3: The Poll
- **Hook:** "What's the biggest bottleneck in your [Workflow]?"
- **Options:** 4 relevant pain points.
- **CTA:** "Vote and tell us why below!"
- **Visual:** None.
