# Real Estate Content Templates

## Instagram (IG)

### RE_IG_1: The Listing Tour
- **Hook:** "Is this your dream kitchen? 😍"
- **Body:** 3 key features of the property (e.g., walk-in closet, view, location).
- **CTA:** "Link in bio for the full tour and price."
- **Visual:** High-quality Reel or Carousel of a beautiful property.

### RE_IG_2: Home Buying Tip
- **Hook:** "First-time buyer? Don't make this mistake! 🛑"
- **Body:** Explain a common pitfall (e.g., hidden costs) and how to avoid it.
- **CTA:** "Save this for your house hunt! 💾"
- **Visual:** Graphic with text overlay.

### RE_IG_3: Neighborhood Spotlight
- **Hook:** "Why [Neighborhood Name] is the place to be! 📍"
- **Body:** Highlight a local park, cafe, and the general vibe.
- **CTA:** "DM for current listings in this area."
- **Visual:** Photo of a local landmark or a cozy street.

## LinkedIn (LI)

### RE_LI_1: Market Analysis
- **Hook:** "[City] Real Estate Market Update: [Month/Year]"
- **Body:** 3 data points (inventory, interest rates, average price) and what they mean for investors.
- **CTA:** "Are you looking to buy or sell this quarter? Let's connect."
- **Visual:** A professional market trend chart.

### RE_LI_2: The Value of Staging
- **Hook:** "Staged homes sell [X]% faster. Here's why."
- **Body:** Discuss the psychology of home buying and why presentation matters.
- **CTA:** "Read my latest blog post for more staging tips."
- **Visual:** A high-quality before/after staging photo.

### RE_LI_3: Investor Insight
- **Hook:** "Why [Specific Type of Property] is a smart play right now."
- **Body:** Analysis of rental yields and long-term appreciation in a specific niche.
- **CTA:** "Contact me for my current 'Off-Market' list."
- **Visual:** Professional headshot or a photo of a multi-unit property.

## Twitter/X (TW)

### RE_TW_1: Interest Rate Watch
- **Hook:** "The Fed just announced..."
- **Body:** One sentence on how it affects mortgage rates.
- **CTA:** "What are you hearing from your lenders?"
- **Visual:** None.

### RE_TW_2: The Real Estate Thread
- **Tweet 1:** "10 questions every home buyer should ask during a viewing. 🧵"
- **Body:** A series of tweets with specific questions (e.g., roof age, neighborhood noise).
- **CTA:** "Follow for daily real estate wisdom."
- **Visual:** Image of a clipboard or a house key.

### RE_TW_3: Rental or Buy?
- **Hook:** "Renting vs. Buying in 2026."
- **Body:** Quick comparison of the pros/cons.
- **CTA:** "Where do you stand? Vote below."
- **Visual:** None.
