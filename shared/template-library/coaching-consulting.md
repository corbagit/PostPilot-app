# Personal Coaching & Consulting Templates

## Instagram (IG)

### COACH_IG_1: The Mindset Shift
- **Hook:** "You're not stuck. You're just [Specific Belief]. ✨"
- **Body:** Explain how changing one thought pattern can lead to massive results.
- **CTA:** "Reply with 'YES' if you're ready to shift! 👇"
- **Visual:** A calming, aesthetic photo of you or a nature scene.

### COACH_IG_2: The Client Win
- **Hook:** "From [Point A] to [Point B] in [Timeframe]. 🚀"
- **Body:** Share a success story (with permission) and the key strategy used.
- **CTA:** "Book a free clarity call in bio."
- **Visual:** A screenshot of a testimonial or a photo of you working with a client.

### COACH_IG_3: Q&A session
- **Hook:** "The #1 question I get asked about [Topic]... 🗣️"
- **Body:** Answer the question clearly and provide one actionable step.
- **CTA:** "What's your question for my next Q&A?"
- **Visual:** A selfie or a video of you talking directly to the camera.

## LinkedIn (LI)

### COACH_LI_1: Business Strategy
- **Hook:** "The biggest mistake [Niche] professionals make is [Problem]."
- **Body:** Breakdown 3 steps to fix the problem and scale more effectively.
- **CTA:** "What strategy are you focusing on this month?"
- **Visual:** A professional chart or a photo of a whiteboard with strategy notes.

### COACH_LI_2: The Power of Networking
- **Hook:** "Your network is your net worth. Here's how I built mine."
- **Body:** 5 tips for authentic networking on LinkedIn.
- **CTA:** "Let's connect! DM me your favorite networking tip."
- **Visual:** A photo of you at a conference or professional event.

### COACH_LI_3: Leadership Lessons
- **Hook:** "Great leaders don't [Action]. They [Action]."
- **Body:** Reflection on what true leadership looks like in a modern workplace.
- **CTA:** "Who is a leader you admire? Tag them below."
- **Visual:** A professional headshot or a photo of a leadership book.

## Twitter/X (TW)

### COACH_TW_1: The Daily Affirmation
- **Hook:** "Today's reminder:"
- **Body:** A short, powerful quote or affirmation for your niche.
- **CTA:** "RT to spread the positive vibes."
- **Visual:** None.

### COACH_TW_2: The Strategy Thread
- **Tweet 1:** "5 ways to increase your productivity by 50%. 🧵"
- **Body:** A thread with actionable tips like time-blocking, deep work, etc.
- **CTA:** "Which one will you try first?"
- **Visual:** None.

### COACH_TW_3: The Challenge
- **Hook:** "I challenge you to [Action] today."
- **Body:** A small, specific challenge for your followers.
- **CTA:** "Reply with 'Done' when you've finished!"
- **Visual:** None.
